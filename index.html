Hi, I’m Dev.

I am currently a graduate student in the Department of Robotics Engineering at Worcester Polytechnic Institute, where I am specializing in the fields of Robot Vision and Artificial Intelligence

Previously, I gained valuable experience as a Computer Vision Engineer Intern at the startup Mowito.in. In this role, I was actively involved in the development of a vision-based software stack for robotic systems.

Furthermore, I had the privilege to contribute to ongoing research at the MOON-Lab within the Indian Institute of Science Education and Research, Bhopal during my tenure as a Research Intern. Under the guidance of Dr. P. B. Sujit, I engaged in advanced research, focusing on the integration of Artificial Intelligence techniques, particularly Reinforcement Learning, with fundamental Control System concepts. I applied these techniques to enhance the performance of robotic systems iteratively.

Throughout my undergraduate studies, I undertook numerous Robotics and AI projects and actively participated in various robotics competitions. You can find details about some of these projects in the project section below.

You can find my full CV and further information regarding my publication and projects below.
